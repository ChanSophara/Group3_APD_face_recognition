How to Use

Camera Test:

  • Click "Start Camera" to begin
  • Position your face in frame
  • Click "Test Recognition" to verify
  • Click "Capture Face" to log test to database
  • Results will show instantly

Upload Test:

  • Click upload area or drag image
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
  • Click "Test Recognition" after upload
  • Automatically logs to database
  • Clear button to start over

Tips:

  • Ensure good lighting
  • Face should be clearly visible
  • Keep face within the overlay area
  • Minimum confidence: 40%
  • Check analytics for performance
